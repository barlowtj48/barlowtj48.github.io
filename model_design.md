# Electric Recliner Repair — Barlow Fabrication

What started as fixing my parents' broken electric recliner has grown into its own business. I design and sell 3D printed replacement parts for broken electric reclining couches and chairs, keeping these assemblies out of the landfill and saving folks money on costly replacements.

This project now has a dedicated home. For the full list of supported models, purchasing options, and to get in touch about a recliner that isn't listed yet, visit:
